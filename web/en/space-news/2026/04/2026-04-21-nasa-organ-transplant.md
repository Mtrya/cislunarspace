# NASA Partners with Organ Sharing Network UNOS to Study Faster Organ Transport

**Summary:** NASA's Langley Research Center has announced a research partnership with the United Network for Organ Sharing (UNOS) to explore how aerospace technologies can optimize high-speed organ transport, aiming to reduce delivery times from donor to recipient and improve transplant success rates.

![NASA and UNOS partnership illustration](./figures/2026-04-21-nasa-organ-transplant/hero.jpg)
*Credit: NASA*

## Research Background

In organ transplantation, time is life. Every hour matters from the moment an organ leaves the donor's body to when it is implanted in the recipient. Traditional ground transportation faces significant challenges when distances are great or traffic is congested. NASA's Langley Research Center, with its extensive expertise in high-speed vehicles, aerodynamics, and life support systems, has partnered with UNOS to apply aerospace technologies to medical transportation.

## Partnership Objectives

This collaboration focuses on several key areas:
- **High-speed transport vehicle design**: Leveraging aerospace aerodynamic and structural design concepts to develop more efficient organ transport containers
- **Temperature control and monitoring**: Utilizing NASA's thermal management systems to ensure organs remain at optimal temperatures throughout transport
- **Real-time tracking systems**: Integrating aerospace communication technologies for continuous position and status monitoring during transport
- **Standardized protocol development**: Establishing new industry standards for medical emergency transport scenarios

## About NASA Langley Research Center

Located in Hampton, Virginia, NASA's Langley Research Center is one of America's oldest aerospace research institutions. The center has extensive experience in aircraft design, flight safety, and high-speed testing, and has been actively pursuing civilian applications of aerospace technologies in recent years.

## About the United Network for Organ Sharing (UNOS)

UNOS is the sole organ procurement and transplantation network (OPO) coordinating organization in the United States, managing the national organ allocation system to ensure fair and efficient organ distribution to patients in need.
