# NASA's University Leadership Initiative Celebrates a Decade of Breakthrough Aviation Innovation

**Summary:** On April 24, 2026, NASA announced the 10th anniversary of its University Leadership Initiative (ULI), which has helped NASA achieve breakthrough innovations in aviation while building the next generation of aviation talent for American industry.

![NASA University Leadership Initiative 10th Anniversary Logo (NASA image)](./figures/2026-04-24-nasa-university-innovation-aeronautics/hero.jpg)
*Credit: NASA*

The University Leadership Initiative (ULI) was launched in 2016 to fully leverage universities' innovative capabilities in aviation research. Over the past decade, the program has driven cutting-edge technology development covering energy-efficient aircraft design, urban air mobility management, advanced materials and manufacturing, and more.

The success of ULI lies in its cross-disciplinary and inter-institutional collaboration model. University teams work closely with NASA researchers, combining academic frontier theories with engineering practice to accelerate the iterative innovation of aviation technology.
