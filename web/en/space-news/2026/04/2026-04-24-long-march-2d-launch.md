# Long March 2D Launch Successful

**Summary:** At 06:25 UTC on April 24, 2026, the Long March 2D (LM-2D) launch vehicle lifted off from the Xichang Satellite Launch Center, successfully placing its payload into the planned orbit. As of publication, detailed payload information has not yet been officially released.

![Long March 2D rocket (file photo)](./figures/2026-04-24-long-march-2d-launch/hero.jpg)
*Credit: TheSpaceDevs*

The Long March 2D launch vehicle is developed by the China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation (CASC). As an improved variant of the Long March 2 series, it is known for its high reliability and adaptability. The rocket is primarily used for launching various Sun-synchronous orbit satellites and can be adapted for different mission requirements.

This launch marks the first launch from Xichang Satellite Launch Center in April 2026, and the 547th flight of the Long March series of launch vehicles.
