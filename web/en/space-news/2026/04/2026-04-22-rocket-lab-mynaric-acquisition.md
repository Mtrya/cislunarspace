# Rocket Lab Completes Mynaric Acquisition, Bolstering Laser Communications Portfolio

**Summary:** Rocket Lab announced the completion of its acquisition of Mynaric, incorporating laser optical communications technology into its growing space systems portfolio. The acquisition will significantly enhance Rocket Lab's ability to provide laser communication terminals for satellite constellations, complementing its existing satellite platform and separation systems business.

![Rocket Lab laser communications concept image](./figures/2026-04-22-rocket-lab-mynaric-acquisition/hero.jpg)
*Credit: Rocket Lab*

## Acquisition Background

Mynaric is a company specializing in laser communication technology, with products including laser communication terminals for high-speed data transmission between satellites and between satellites and ground stations. As satellite constellations continue to scale up, demand for laser communications is growing, as laser communications offer higher bandwidth and lower latency compared to traditional RF communications.

Rocket Lab has been providing customers with satellite manufacturing and launch services through its satellite platform business. After acquiring Mynaric, Rocket Lab will be able to provide customers with complete solutions from satellite platforms to communication terminals.

## Rocket Lab Space Systems Business Expansion

Rocket Lab has been actively expanding its space systems business in recent years, forming a diversified product line including rocket launch, satellite platforms, payload separation systems, and laser communication terminals. Earlier in April 2026, Rocket Lab also secured a $19 million HASTE hypersonic suborbital launch contract with the US Department of Defense, further strengthening its market position in the defense and research launch sectors.
