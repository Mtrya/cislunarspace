# NASA Wins Two Webby Awards and Five People's Voice Awards at 30th Annual Ceremony

**Summary:** On April 21, 2026, NASA was honored with two Webby Awards and five People's Voice Awards at the 30th Annual Webby Awards, one of the internet's most prestigious recognition programs. The awards highlight NASA's outstanding digital communications, online content, and public engagement efforts across its websites and digital platforms.

![NASA received multiple Webby Awards in 2026 for its digital content and online platforms (NASA photo)](./figures/2026-04-22-nasa-webby-awards/hero.jpg)
*Credit: NASA*

## NASA's Digital Excellence Recognized

The Webby Awards, often called the "Internet's Highest Honor," are awarded annually by the International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences. NASA's recognition at the 30th Annual Webby Awards underscores the agency's continued leadership in science communication and its ability to engage the public with compelling, accessible content about space exploration and Earth science.

The five People's Voice Awards, which are determined by public vote, reflect the public's enthusiasm for NASA's online content and its commitment to sharing the wonders of space and science with everyone.

## Full List of NASA's 30th Annual Webby Award Wins

NASA's award-winning content spanned multiple categories, including:

- **Science Education**: Interactive features and multimedia content explaining complex NASA missions
- **Earth Science Communications**: Updates and imagery from NASA's Earth observation satellites
- **Social Media and Outreach**: NASA's engagement across its digital platforms, including websites, social media, and multimedia content

## About the Webby Awards

The Webby Awards are presented annually by the International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences, a global organization of technology pioneers, artists, and cultural leaders. The awards recognize excellence on the internet across multiple categories, including websites, advertising, media, mobile apps, and online video content. The People's Voice Awards are determined by public voting and run concurrently with the main Webby Awards.

NASA's consistent recognition at the Webby Awards reflects the agency's longstanding commitment to making its missions, research, and discoveries accessible to the public in engaging and innovative ways.
