# NASA Invests $16.3 Million to Support Small and Medium Space Enterprises

**Summary:** On April 21, 2026, NASA announced approximately $16.3 million in seed funding to over 30 American small businesses through its Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) and Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) programs. The awards support the development of innovative technologies for lunar exploration, Mars missions, and life on Earth. The new awards include NASA's SBIR Ignite initiative and traditional SBIR/STTR programs, aimed at stimulating commercialization and strengthening America's space economy.
