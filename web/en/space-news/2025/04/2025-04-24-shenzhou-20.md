# Shenzhou 20 Crewed Spacecraft Successfully Launched

**Summary:** On April 24, 2025 (Beijing Time), China successfully launched the Shenzhou 20 crewed spacecraft from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center aboard a Long March 2F carrier rocket, sending a crew of astronauts to the Tiangong Space Station. The spacecraft successfully docked with the space station, and the crew entered the orbital outpost to begin their on-orbit mission. Shenzhou 20 was the first crewed spaceflight mission of China's manned space program in 2025, and the 565th flight of the Long March series of launch vehicles.
