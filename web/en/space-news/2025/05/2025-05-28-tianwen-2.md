# Tianwen-2 Probe Successfully Launched

**Summary:** On May 28, 2025 (Beijing Time), China's Tianwen-2 probe was successfully launched aboard a Long March 3B carrier rocket from the Xichang Satellite Launch Center. Tianwen-2 is China's first asteroid sample return mission — the probe will conduct close-range observation and collect samples from a near-Earth asteroid, with the samples planned to be returned to Earth by 2030. This mission will provide crucial scientific data for understanding the origin and evolution of the solar system.
