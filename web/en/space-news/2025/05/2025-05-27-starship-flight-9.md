# SpaceX Starship Flight 9 Experiences Setback

**Summary:** On May 27, 2025 (Eastern Time), SpaceX conducted the ninth integrated flight test of Starship (IFT-9). The rocket experienced an anomaly during flight and did not complete all planned mission objectives. SpaceX stated that the team was analyzing the descent sequence and would continue to advance the flight test program in subsequent missions. The Starship development process has always been centered on iterative testing, with each flight providing valuable data toward the ultimate goal of full reusability.
