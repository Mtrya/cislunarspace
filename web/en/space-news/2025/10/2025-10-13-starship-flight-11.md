# SpaceX Starship Flight 11 Achieves Success

**Summary:** On October 13, 2025 (Eastern Time), SpaceX conducted the eleventh integrated flight test of Starship (IFT-11) from the Starbase in Boca Chica, Texas. This flight successfully completed all planned objectives — the Super Heavy booster achieved a successful landing recovery at the launch site, and the Starship spacecraft completed its planned controlled splashdown. IFT-11 continued the success trajectory of IFT-10, marking Starship's steady progress toward the goal of full reusability.
