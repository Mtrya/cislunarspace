# Zhuque-3 Rocket Achieves Maiden Flight Success

**Summary:** On December 3, 2025 (Beijing Time), the Zhuque-3 carrier rocket developed by LandSpace was successfully launched on its maiden flight from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center. Zhuque-3 is an important milestone in China's commercial space sector, utilizing liquid oxygen-methane engine technology, representing the development direction of reusable rocket engines. The successful maiden flight further promoted the development of China's commercial space industry.
