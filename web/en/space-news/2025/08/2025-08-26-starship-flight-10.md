# SpaceX Starship Flight 10 Achieves Success

**Summary:** On August 26, 2025 (Eastern Time), SpaceX conducted the tenth integrated flight test of Starship (IFT-10) from the Starbase in Boca Chica, Texas. This flight achieved all primary objectives — the Super Heavy booster successfully landed back at the launch site, and the Starship spacecraft achieved a controlled splashdown. The success of IFT-10 further validated the effectiveness of SpaceX's rapid iterative development approach and laid the foundation for subsequent crewed lunar landing missions.
