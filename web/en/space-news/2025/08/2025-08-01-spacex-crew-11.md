# SpaceX Crew-11 Successfully Launched

**Summary:** On August 1, 2025 (Eastern Time), SpaceX successfully launched the Crew-11 Crew Dragon mission from Launch Complex 39A at Kennedy Space Center, carrying 4 astronauts to the International Space Station for an approximately 6-month on-orbit mission. Crew-11 was the 11th mission in SpaceX's commercial crew program and an important component of NASA's Commercial Crew Program. This launch marked SpaceX's Crew Dragon as entering a mature operational phase.
