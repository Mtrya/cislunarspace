# Shenzhou 22 Crewed Spacecraft Successfully Launched

**Summary:** On November 25, 2025 (Beijing Time), China's Shenzhou 22 crewed spacecraft was successfully launched from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center aboard a Long March 2F carrier rocket, sending a new crew of astronauts to the Tiangong Space Station. The spacecraft successfully docked with the space station, and the astronauts entered the orbital outpost to begin their mission. The Shenzhou 22 mission marked the transition of Tiangong Space Station operations into a normalized phase, with the crew rotation mechanism running continuously.
