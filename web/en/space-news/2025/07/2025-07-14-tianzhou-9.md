# Tianzhou-9 Cargo Spacecraft Successfully Launched

**Summary:** On July 14, 2025 (Beijing Time), China's Tianzhou-9 cargo spacecraft was successfully launched aboard a Long March 7 carrier rocket from the Wenchang Space Launch Site. The spacecraft subsequently docked with the Tiangong Space Station, delivering supplies, fuel, and scientific experimental equipment to the orbiting astronauts. Tianzhou-9 is a critical resupply mission for the Tiangong Space Station, supporting the long-term on-orbit operations of the orbital outpost.
