# Navigation & Orbit Determination

## Background

Cislunar space presents new challenges for navigation due to long distances, weak signals, and complex dynamics.

## Research Areas

- GNSS weak signal navigation
- Pulsar navigation
- Inter-satellite ranging and orbit determination
- Autonomous navigation algorithms
