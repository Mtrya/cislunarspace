# True Anomaly

## Company Overview

True Anomaly is a U.S. space defense technology startup founded around 2020, headquartered in Colorado. True Anomaly is currently the only private company focused exclusively on the space defense domain. Its mission is to provide the United States and its allies with the capabilities needed to achieve decisive advantage in space. The company completed a $100 million financing round in December 2024 but immediately conducted layoffs of approximately one-quarter of its workforce to streamline operations. The company is rapidly building its space vehicles and ground systems, aiming to become one of the core Tactical Response Space (TacRS) providers for the U.S. Space Force.

## Space and Cislunar-Related Business

### Jackal Autonomous Orbital Vehicle

True Anomaly's core product is the Jackal autonomous orbital vehicle:

- **Positioning**: Jackal is an autonomous satellite platform used for Space Domain Awareness (SDA) and Proximity Operations (PROX).
- **Mission Capability**: Jackal can autonomously approach target spacecraft and execute close-range observation, imaging, and intelligence collection missions, supporting the needs of the U.S. Space Force and intelligence agencies. In the second mission in 2024, True Anomaly successfully deployed the Jackal satellite and established communications, completing a key milestone.
- **Swarm Coordination**: True Anomaly is developing multi-satellite cooperative operations capability, supporting multiple Jackal vehicles simultaneously executing complex orbital proximity and sensing missions.

### Tactically Responsive Space (TacRS)

True Anomaly is actively participating in the U.S. Space Force's Tactically Responsive Space (TacRS) program:

- **Victus Haze Mission**: In 2024, True Anomaly announced it would support the military's Victus Haze mission, planned to execute in 2025 as part of the TacRS program. Firefly Aerospace's Alpha rocket was selected as the launch platform for the Jackal vehicle.
- **Rapid Response Capability**: The core goal of TacRS is to demonstrate rapid response capability — completing missions from assignment to launch in a few days to weeks, without the traditional preparation cycle of months.

### Space Domain Awareness (SDA)

True Anomaly provides high-resolution Space Domain Awareness data through its Jackal platform:

- **Close-Range Observation**: Through proximity operations, Jackal can perform high-resolution optical imaging of target satellites, obtaining detailed configuration and status information.
- **Orbital Behavior Analysis**: Long-term tracking and analysis of target satellite orbital maneuver behavior to identify anomalous activity patterns.
- **Cislunar Space Potential**: Although Jackal currently operates primarily in LEO, its platform capability can conceptually be extended to higher cislunar space orbits.

## Connection to Cislunar Space Missions

True Anomaly's connection to the cislunar space domain is primarily at the defense situational awareness level:

- **Extended Tracking Capability**: Its orbital proximity and imaging technology, after improvement, can be applied to cislunar space space object tracking, filling the current gap in cislunar SDA capabilities.
- **Orbital Countermeasure Capability**: The orbital proximity and maneuvering capabilities True Anomaly is developing can, in confrontation scenarios, be used for cislunar space precision strikes akin to orbital surgery (see the "Orbital Game" entry).
- **Rapid Response Architecture**: The rapid response concept of TacRS is significant for the protection of high-value assets in cislunar space, enabling deployment of sensors or countermeasure payloads within days.
