## Company Overview

Booz Allen Hamilton (commonly referred to as BAH) is a U.S. management consulting and technology services firm founded in 1914, headquartered in McLean, Virginia. BAH is one of the primary IT and consulting service providers for the U.S. Department of Defense, Department of Homeland Security, and U.S. Space Force. Its business covers cyber operations, space systems support, intelligence analysis, and logistics management. In the space domain, BAH is renowned for its software engineering capabilities, data analytics capabilities, and systems integration capabilities, and has been deeply involved in multiple U.S. Space Force digital transformation projects.

## Space and Cislunar-Related Business

### Space Situational Awareness (SSA)

BAH is one of the primary contractors for the U.S. Space Force's Space Situational Awareness (SSA) system:

- Provides software systems development and technical support for the operation and maintenance of the Joint Space Operations Center (JpOC) for the U.S. Space Force.
- Develops data fusion platforms for space object tracking, integrating ground-based radar, optical telescope, and space-based sensor data.
- Supports the design of data standards and information sharing architecture for Space Traffic Management (STM).

### Cislunar Navigation Support

BAH has participated in multiple cislunar navigation-related research projects:

- Supports NASA's cislunar navigation architecture research, exploring the feasibility of weak-signal GNSS receivers in cislunar space.
- Participates in NASA projects such as LunaNet, providing systems integration and software engineering support.

### Digital Space Force Development

BAH plays an important role in the U.S. Space Force's digital transformation:

- Develops IT infrastructure and cloud platforms for the Space Force.
- Provides cybersecurity assessment and threat detection services.
- Supports the Space Force Software Factory project.

## Potential Connection to Cislunar Space Missions

Booz Allen Hamilton's role in the cislunar space domain is primarily at the infrastructure and software services level:

- **Navigation Architecture**: Its participation in weak-signal GNSS navigation and LunaNet projects positions it at the forefront of cislunar navigation architecture research.
- **Space Traffic Management**: BAH's research on STM data standards and information sharing will directly influence the formulation of operating rules for cislunar space in the future.
- **Situational Awareness**: Cislunar space object tracking and cataloging requires massive data fusion capabilities, and the systems BAH has developed in the SSA domain can be extended to cislunar space.
