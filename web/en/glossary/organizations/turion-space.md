## Company Overview

Turion Space is a U.S. space technology startup focused on space debris removal, orbital transfer, and Space Situational Awareness (SSA). The company is developing and operating a versatile orbital transfer platform — DROID (Dirty ROtating Instrument for Debris Observation and elimination) — designed to provide cost-effective space debris removal and orbital service solutions for commercial and government customers. Turion Space's technology approach focuses on low to medium Earth orbit (LEO/MEO) regions, with the goal of becoming a significant participant in the on-orbit services market as the space debris problem grows increasingly severe.

## Space and Cislunar-Related Business

### DROID Orbital Transfer Platform

Turion Space's core product is the DROID satellite platform series:

- **DROID.002 Mission**: In 2024, Turion Space successfully launched the DROID.002 satellite — an on-orbit verification mission for its versatile orbital transfer platform. The DROID platform is designed to perform orbital rendezvous and close-proximity operations with target spacecraft, supporting debris grasping, orbital transfer, and status inspection missions.
- **Rendezvous and Proximity Operations (RPO)**: The DROID platform has precise orbital maneuvering capability, can autonomously approach target objects, and execute close-range observation or capture operations.
- **Debris Removal**: One of DROID's primary application scenarios is active removal of defunct satellites and large debris, using mechanical grasping or net capture to drag targets out of their original orbits.

### Space Situational Awareness (SSA) Services

Turion Space provides space situational awareness data services through its platform:

- **On-Orbit Status Monitoring**: The DROID platform can perform close-range observation of target satellites, obtaining detailed information such as configuration changes, solar panel deployment status, and antenna pointing.
- **Collision Warning**: Based on close-range observation data, provides higher-precision collision risk assessment and warning.
- **Cislunar Space SSA**: While current missions primarily focus on LEO/MEO, Turion Space has included cislunar orbital region SSA services in its long-term roadmap.

### Commercial Orbital Services

- **Orbital Upward and Downward Transfer**: The DROID platform can serve as a "space tugboat," moving satellites from operational orbits to disposal orbits, or vice versa.
- **Multi-Customer Services**: A single DROID platform can serve multiple satellites in a single mission, reducing unit service costs.

## Connection to Cislunar Space Missions

Turion Space's technology connects to cislunar space in the following ways:

- **Debris Removal Extension**: Experience and technology from LEO debris removal can be adapted for cislunar space debris problems after modification. As cislunar space activity increases, debris accumulation will become an increasingly prominent issue.
- **Value Satellite Maintenance**: High-value assets in cislunar space (such as Lunar Gateway modules and NRHO platforms) can benefit from Turion Space's on-orbit inspection and orbital service capabilities.
- **Orbital Maneuvering Capability**: The orbital rendezvous and proximity operations capability demonstrated by the DROID platform is the technical foundation for cislunar space on-orbit services, which can be extended to mission support in the cislunar L1/NRHO region in the future.
