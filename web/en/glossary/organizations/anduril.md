## Company Overview

Anduril Industries is a U.S. defense technology company founded in 2017 by Palmer Luckey (founder of Oculus), headquartered in Irvine, California. Anduril's mission is to "rebuild the defense industry from within the firewall" by applying commercial software development methodologies to rapid weapon system iteration. The company has grown into one of the most important private suppliers to the U.S. military. In the space domain, Anduril's product lines include satellite platforms, orbital robots, attitude control systems, and software-defined networks.

## Space and Cislunar-Related Business

### Satellite Platforms and Attitude Control

Anduril has developed multiple hardware products for space missions:

- **Attitude Determination and Control System (ADCS)**: Anduril provides high-precision attitude determination and control system hardware solutions for its satellite platforms, supporting Earth observation, communications, and orbit-keeping missions.
- **Prometheus Engine**: A 3D-printed reusable rocket engine with adjustable thrust, supporting orbital transfer and attitude control.

### Orbital Robotics and On-Orbit Services

Anduril has built an orbital robotics business through acquisitions:

- **Rover Robots**: Anduril is developing rover-type robotic systems for on-orbit satellite servicing and debris removal, supporting close-proximity operations and robotic arm grasping with target spacecraft.
- Through the acquisition of certain assets from Sierra Nevada Corporation, Anduril gained **additional** on-orbit service and manufacturing capabilities.

### Software-Defined Combat Network

- **Lattice Platform**: Anduril's core software product, initially used for ground-based UAV swarm command and control, has expanded into the space situational awareness (SSA) domain, supporting multi-satellite cooperative detection and tracking.

## Connection to Cislunar Space Missions

Anduril is competing for multiple on-orbit service contracts with the U.S. Space Force and NASA, and its technological capabilities relate to the following cislunar space scenarios:

- **Debris Removal and On-Orbit Servicing**: Orbital robotics technology can be extended to handle retired spacecraft in cislunar space.
- **Space Situational Awareness**: Lattice platform's data fusion capabilities can be applied to cislunar space traffic management.
- **Logistics Support**: Reusable propulsion technology is a foundational capability for future cislunar cargo systems.
