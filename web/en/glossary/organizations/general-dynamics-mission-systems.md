## Company Overview

General Dynamics Mission Systems (GDMS) is a subsidiary of General Dynamics Corporation, specializing in mission systems integration for the defense and intelligence markets. GDMS was formerly known as General Dynamics Advanced Information Systems (GD-AIS) and was renamed to its current name in 2012, headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona. GDMS is one of the primary suppliers of U.S. Navy underwater warfare systems, Army tactical communication systems, and space ground systems. In January 2025, China's Ministry of Commerce added General Dynamics Mission Systems to its export control list, prohibiting the export of dual-use items to the company.

## Space and Cislunar-Related Business

### Space Architecture and Ground Systems

GDMS is active in the Space Architecture domain and is one of the primary contractors for the U.S. Space Development Agency (SDA):

- **Space Architecture Development**: Participates in the ground segment development for the National Defense Space Architecture (NDSA) system, including data processing, transmission, and secure communications.
- **Missile Tracking**: Supports the Space Force's Next Generation Overhead Persistent Infrared (Next Gen OPIR) system's ground data processing.

### Communications Systems

GDMS is a significant supplier of tactical communications and satellite ground station systems:

- **Satellite Ground Stations**: Develops and maintains multi-band satellite communications ground stations, supporting high-frequency satellite communications systems such as Q/V band.
- **Secure Networks**: Provides anti-jamming, low-probability-of-intercept military satellite communications solutions.

### Intelligence and Data Processing

- **Intelligence Processing Systems**: Provides signals intelligence (SIGINT) and imagery intelligence processing systems for the U.S. National Reconnaissance Office (NRO) and intelligence agencies.
- **AI-Assisted Analysis**: Integrates AI/ML capabilities into intelligence data processing to support rapid analysis of large-scale satellite data.

## Potential Connection to Cislunar Space Missions

GDMS's contributions to the cislunar space domain are primarily at the infrastructure level:

- **Communications Backbone**: Its satellite ground station technology is a critical component of the cislunar space communications network, and high-frequency satellite communications technology can support high-capacity data transmission between Earth and the Moon.
- **Situational Awareness Data Processing**: Its intelligence processing platform can be adapted to process cislunar space object tracking data.
- **Secure Communications**: Cislunar space military and civilian communications require high-security-grade encryption and anti-jamming solutions.
